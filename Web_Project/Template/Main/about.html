About Us


MERI COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

MERI College Of Engineering and Technologyis one of the Top Engineering College in Delhi NCR region. It is a part of MERI Group of Institutions which is in the field of education since 1987. The main campus of the group is in Delhi. The NCR campus of the College offers Engineering courses at B.Tech and M.Tech Level duly affiliated to MDU, Rohtak. The college has been ranked as a top upcoming engineering college for last many years by EFY, a prestigious engineering magazine in India. The campus is located in lush green pollution free environment at Bahadurgarh, in Delhi NCR and is just 20 minutes drive from Delhi on its western border. It has a spacious building with a corporate look and state of the art labs, Wi-Fi campus, air-conditioned class rooms and excellent and world-class infrastructure.
